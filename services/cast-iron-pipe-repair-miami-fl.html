Frequently Asked Questions

How long do cast iron pipes typically last in Miami?

Cast iron pipes typically last 50-75 years in Miami's climate. However, due to soil conditions and salt exposure, many pipes installed before 1975 are now showing signs of deterioration and may need replacement.

What are the signs of failing cast iron pipes?

Common signs include slow drains, frequent clogs, foul odors, water backing up, foundation cracks, and wet spots in yard. You might also notice rusty water or hear gurgling sounds.

Do you offer financing for cast iron pipe replacement?

Yes, we offer flexible financing options with approved credit. We also work directly with insurance companies for claims related to cast iron pipe damage.

How long does cast iron pipe replacement take?

Most residential cast iron pipe replacements take 2-5 days, depending on the scope of work. Emergency repairs can often be completed within 24 hours.
