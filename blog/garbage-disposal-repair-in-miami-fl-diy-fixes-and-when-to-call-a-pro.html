DIY Garbage Disposal Fixes You Can Try

Before calling for professional help, try these safe DIY solutions:

1. Reset Button Method

Look for the red reset button on the bottom of the disposal unit. Press it firmly and wait 10 minutes before testing.

2. Allen Wrench Technique

Insert the appropriately sized Allen wrench into the bottom hole and turn it back and forth to dislodge debris.

3. Natural Cleaning Solution

Pour 1/2 cup baking soda followed by 1/2 cup vinegar. Wait 15 minutes, then flush with hot water.

Follow these maintenance tips to avoid common issues:
